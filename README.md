News Credibility Analyzer

What is this?
This is a simple tool to check if local news is trustworthy. It looks for author names, official sources, and checks if the tone is too angry or emotional.

How to use it:
1. Copy the text from a news article.
2. Paste it into the box on the website.
3. Click **Analyze News** to see your score!

Project Goals:
* To help people trust local news.
* To find and flag "clickbait" or angry news.
